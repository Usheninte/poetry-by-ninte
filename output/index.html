Lucid

Published: Sat 05 December 2020
By Usheninte Dangana

In Life.

clear are my thoughts
after wars won, and love lost
succeed we must
as long as air kisses the dust

my art will grow
joy you will know
for we are the light
of an ancient sun

strong we shall be
this be our destiny
