Mixed

Published: Fri 04 December 2020
By Usheninte Dangana

In Affection.

I know what I feel
you know that it's real
unsure? no big deal
I hurts to still feel

to bend not my ways
and break not your rays
I'll walk same as always
away with dark grace

I will let you go
so joy you will know
you need not be sad
for a while we were glad
