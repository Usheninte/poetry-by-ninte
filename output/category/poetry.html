At an end

Published: Tue 29 December 2020
By Usheninte Dangana

In Poetry.

fight the void
or fight the noise
either way, it is your choice

the darkness
is the light
the bright star
shines not at night

ask why
of yourself, and I
will appear and
teach you of
ancient philosophies

pause here,
make no love to hypocrisies
