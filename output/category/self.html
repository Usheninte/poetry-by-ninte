Alone

Published: Mon 30 November 2020
By Usheninte Dangana

In Self.

look at the title
I am not so
speak and breathe with apostles
dance around thoughts colossal

I am a child of God so
I fall, I weep, I think
to live, is not to be good or bad, so
I learn my ways
each passing day
