Hello

Published: Sat 28 November 2020
By Usheninte Dangana

In Affection.

we are strangers
who know each other
in our generation
that's the most normal thing

sing a tune for your soul
say hello, and some words more
to the face you used to know
if you didn't care then
do not so now
