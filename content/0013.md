Title: At an end
Date: 2020-12-29 13:15
Category: Poetry

fight the void  
or fight the noise  
either way, it is your choice

the darkness  
is the light  
the bright star  
shines not at night

ask why  
of yourself, and I  
will appear and  
teach you of  
ancient philosophies

pause here,  
make no love to hypocrisies
